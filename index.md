## 🔍 Troubleshooting Common Issues

Check this section if you encounter errors during operation.

**The tool does not detect your iPhone:**

*   Ensure you used a solid data cable. Charging-only cables do not transfer data.
*   Check if you installed the Apple Mobile Device Support drivers.
*   Try a different USB port on your computer, preferably one on the back of the case if you use a desktop PC.

**The software crashes during the process:**

*   Disable your antivirus software temporarily. Some security tools view the bypass process as a threat.
*   Ensure you run the program with administrative rights. Right-click the icon and choose "Run as Administrator."
*   Verify that you have enough disk space on your drive.

**The progress bar stops at 10%:**

*   Place your device into DFU mode. To do this, turn off the device, press and hold the power and home/volume buttons, then release the power button while continuing to hold the other button until the computer recognizes the device.
*   Close any background applications that might conflict with the tool.
